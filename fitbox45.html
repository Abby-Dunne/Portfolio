Fitbox45 Website Design

ROLE: website designer

TEAM: a ux lead, business lead, other UI designer

COMPANY: EnticEdge

GOAL: Design a user-friendly, organized website for clientsâ€™ boxing training services

Process

Since the client wanted to use Wix website builder, I researched the prices and looked at various templates for gym websites to get an idea of what is typically included. We had an initial meeting with a client to figure out the pricing plan and to discuss potential content for the page.

We figured out a page hierarchy first. To generate ideas I sketched out some possible layouts on paper before proceeding to digital designs. I then created low-fi digital wireframes in Figma (this was my first time creating a digital wireframe).

Once we had the wireframe layouts, we had a meeting with the client to discuss the designs and make any changes or redesigns before going forward. I wanted to know what information they thought was most important for the homepage.

The lead UI / graphic designer proposed color schemes for the websites and we discussed them with the lead ux designer. We ended up using both the red and yellow as accent colors as it gave a more distinct brand.

We applied the color scheme to the layouts to produce some high-fidelity designs.

Once we had them it was time to prototype the navigation in figma so we could have a working model of the website to show the client the final design before it is ready to publish. The client approved, and it was ready to publish on wix. My work here was done.

Outcome

A refreshed website that provides booking services, a shopping platform, and information on the training facility and staff.

Here is the final website: https://www.fbx45.com/
